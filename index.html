Instructions

Welcome to The Scottish Quiz! Here you can test your knowledge of beautiful Bonnie Scotland. However before diving in there are a few things you should know...

  • The quiz consists of 10 randomly selected multiple choice questions

  • Once you click your answer it can't be undone, so choose wisely!

  • If during the quiz you wish to return to the home screen click the castle icon at the top of the page

  • Your score will be displayed as the quiz progresses

  • At the end of the quiz create a username to add your score to the leaderboard

Good Luck
