Instructions

Welcome to The Scottish Quiz! Here you can test your knowledge of beautiful Bonnie Scotland. However before diving in there is a few things you should know...

  • The quiz consists of 5 randomly selected multiple choice questions
  • Once you click your answer it can't be undone, so choose wisely!
  • You will have 10 seconds to answer each question
  • Your score will be displayed as the quiz progresses
  • At the end of the quiz create a username to add your score to the leaderboard
Good Luck!
